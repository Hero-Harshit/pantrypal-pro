Common Questions

Quick answers to our most frequently asked questions.

How do I cancel my subscription? ▼
You can cancel anytime via the 'Profile' section in your dashboard. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle.
Is my payment data secure? ▼
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and processed through certified third-party payment gateways. We never store your card details on our servers.
Can I switch from Plus to Pro mid-month? ▼
Absolutely! When you upgrade, we calculate the remaining value of your current plan and apply it as a discount to your new subscription.
I found a bug — how do I report it? ▼
We love feedback! Use the form above and select 'Bug Report' as the subject. Including steps to reproduce the issue helps us fix it faster.
