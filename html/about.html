About Us

Pantry to Plate, Perfected.

Our Mission

We exist to eliminate food waste and make cooking joyful for everyone. In an era where convenience often comes at the cost of sustainability, we're bringing the focus back to the heart of the home: the kitchen.

Every Indian household throws away food not because they want to — but because they don't know what to make with those leftover veggies or that half-empty jar of dal. We fix that by turning your pantry into a playground of culinary possibilities.

Our Story

Founded in 2026 by Hero Harshit, a food lover and engineer from Pune, PantryPal Pro began as a simple side project born out of personal frustration. Watching good ingredients go to waste after a long week of work felt like a problem technology could solve.

What started as a tool for friends quickly grew into a full product when we realized the scale of the issue. The "aha moment" was realizing the average Indian family wastes ₹3,000 worth of food per month. We knew we had to build something that wasn't just smart, but truly helpful for busy Indian homes.
